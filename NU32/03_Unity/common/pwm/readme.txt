irda_dummy.c - Tested different scenarios:

    Beacons received from the IrDA Pic:
    \nBTx CRC\r         argc = 2            \nMSx CRC\r         argc = 2
    \nBCx 1234 CRC\r    argc = 3            \nMRx 1234 CRC\r    argc = 3

    \nATx 1234 CRC\r    argc = 4            \nASx 1234 CRC\r    argc = 4
